Awareness about digitalised money transaction


Objective:Educating people regarding how to transfer money using mobile apps and Helping people to install and use digital payment apps.

We visited Mydala village of tumkur. We educated village people about the benifits of digital payments. We taught how to use google pay, phone pay and other useful mobile apps. We provided information about online money transaction to local shops and asked them to switch to digital mode of payment options like QR scanners.

Outcomes:Digitalization enables easier and wider access to various services, such as healthcare, education, banking, government services and e-commerce.
